I love Yogurt

Especially Greek Yogurt

Strained yogurt, Greek yogurt, yogurt cheese, or sack yoghurt is yogurt that has been strained to remove most of its whey, resulting in a thicker consistency than unstrained yogurt, while preserving yogurt's distinctive sour taste.

Yogurts are considered a healthy food choice for several reasons:

  • Rich in Nutrients: Yogurt is packed with essential nutrients like calcium, which is crucial for bone health, as well as vitamins B6 and B12, riboflavin, potassium, and magnesium.
  • High in Protein: Yogurt, especially Greek yogurt, is a great source of protein, which is vital for many bodily processes, including muscle repair and metabolism.
  • Probiotics for Gut Health: Many yogurts contain live bacteria or probiotics, which can aid in digestion, improve gut health, and even boost the immune system.
  • May Boost Immune System: Some studies suggest that the probiotics in yogurt can help strengthen the immune system, partly due to the increased production of antibodies and a boost to immune cells.
